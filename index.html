How to Play

Don't let the pirate kittens take over your island!

  • Type the words on the pirate ships to collect coins and protect the island
  • The longer the word, the higher your the score
  • If a pirate kitten reaches the island, it will place a flag and you will lose a life
  • The game ends when you lose all three lives or the time runs out
  • Push ENTER to clear your entire input
